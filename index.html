Our Story

A Table for Every Craving

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Hazratganj, Mantar is Lucknow's most eclectic dining destination — a place where sizzling Indo-Chinese woks meet Awadhi dum biryanis, where wood-fired tandoors share space with crispy momos and wood-baked pizzas.

Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a romantic dinner, or a lively evening with friends, Mantar wraps you in warmth, flavour, and the unmistakable spirit of Lucknow.
